# Lena Castillo — creative-media pack persona

> **Fictional.** Sample persona for the creative-media role pack ([`../../SPEC-role-packs.md`](../../SPEC-role-packs.md) §3). Handles carry the `-fictional` suffix, same convention as `assets/fixtures/persona-jane-doe/`. Persona id: `lena`.

## Who she is

Lena Castillo, 34, video editor and motion designer in Brooklyn, freelance since 2020 under Studio Castillo. Twelve years of cutting: 120+ published cuts as an assistant at VICE, three videos past 5M views as an editor at Vox, twenty-nine client deliveries since going independent — nine of them repeat clients, which is the number she leads with when a producer asks if she's easy to work with. Her short documentary *Night Shift* (editor) made a Tribeca shortlist in 2023.

## The arc

Film & TV at NYU Tisch, then the assistant-editor years at VICE — volume, deadlines, and someone else's style, 120+ cuts of it. At Vox (2017–2020) she got her own cuts and her range: explainers, mini-docs, motion-heavy social formats. Freelance since late 2020, deliberately split between brand work (the rent) and documentary (the reason). Her 2026 reel is 75 seconds, twelve projects, best ten seconds first — and every clip carries a contribution label (*edit*, *edit + motion*, *motion only*), because collaborative work claimed whole is the fastest way to lose a reviewer who checks.

## Why the creative-media pack fits

Reviewers decide in the first 10 seconds of the reel, frequently watching on mute — so the reel leads the page as a baked Vimeo thumbnail linking out, and it's cut mute-proof. An uncurated book loses the reviewer immediately; Lena ships eight pieces, not thirty, with deliberate range across doc, brand, and motion (one-style lock reads "hard to direct"). Client-approved work does double duty: it proves the work *and* proves she survives revision cycles. The pack's rights gate shapes what's absent — client footage appears only with permission, no unlicensed music claims, and one embargoed brand campaign stays off the page entirely until the embargo lifts. Minimal copy throughout: the work talks, the captions attribute.
