## Why the consultant pack fits

Consulting screeners give a résumé 7–11 seconds: visual structure, prestige markers, then the first bullet of the most recent role — weak first bullet and they stop. Yusuf's page is built for that pass: dense quantified bullets, anonymized engagement cards with credible specificity, and a visible "client names withheld" note that reads as professionalism rather than evasion. This is the highest-NDA-density pack and he plays it disciplined: anything non-public is assumed confidential, deltas are relative, and the page offers "fuller detail on request" instead of baking confidential material in — because over-sharing is the disqualifier, and a bare "can't share, NDA" placeholder page is the other one. Frameworks over screenshots; prose-led throughout.
